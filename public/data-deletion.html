Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 21, 2026

You have the right to delete your ResistAct account and the data associated with it at any time. Here's how.

Option 1 — Email us

  1. Send an email to lnmezz@gmail.com from the address linked to your ResistAct account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my ResistAct account".
  3. We'll confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

Option 2 — If you signed in with Facebook

You can revoke ResistAct's access from your Facebook account:

  1. Go to Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites.
  2. Find ResistAct in the list and click Remove.
  3. Facebook will stop sharing your data with us. To also delete your ResistAct account records, follow Option 1 above.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email, avatar).
  • Your posted content (acts and facts you authored), unless it has been reshared and we need to retain it for legitimate moderation or legal purposes.
  • Your boosts, bookmarks, and activity records.
Note: Server-side backups may retain a copy for up to 30 days after deletion before they roll over. After that point, no data about you remains in our systems.
